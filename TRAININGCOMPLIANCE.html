The Psych area is a specialized department within the immigration law firm responsible for conducting in-depth interviews with clients. Its primary function is to gather detailed, first-hand information to evaluate whether the client meets the eligibility criteria for the specific immigration visa being pursued.

El área de Psych es un departamento especializado dentro de la firma legal migratoria responsable de conducir entrevistas a profundidad con los clientes.
