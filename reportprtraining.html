Session SummaryDay 1
Theory review applied to cases

The session began with a brief T Visa theory review — eligibility elements, types of trafficking, and the EMP model — oriented directly toward practical application, connecting concepts to real client scenarios without dwelling on pure theory.

Participants listened to DVS intake call recordings and extracted EMP elements to complete trafficking analysis forms. Leonardo Pazos and Lourdes Zapata contributed key observations during the recordings review, sharing direct client experience that enriched the group's analysis.

Key improvement areas identified: deepen recruitment mechanism from the first call, systematically assess hardship, and document specific threats with greater precision.
